# Q6-8

$$X_1,\cdots,X_n,i.i.d.\sim Po(\lambda)$$
$$\lambda\sim Ga(\alpha,\beta)$$

# A1

$$\lambda|y\sim Ga(y+\alpha,\frac{\beta}{1+n\beta})$$

# A2

$$E[\lambda|y]=\frac{\beta(y+\alpha)}{1+n\beta}$$

$$Var(\lambda|y)=\frac{(y+\alpha)\beta^2}{(n\beta+1)^2}$$
